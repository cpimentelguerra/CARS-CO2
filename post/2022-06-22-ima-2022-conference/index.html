IMA 2022 conference

Last updated on Jun 22, 2022 1 min read

Good news! First results of CARS-CO2 project will be presented at IMA2022 conference in Lyon (France).

The talk is entitled Monitoring CaSO4 carbonation with in situ Raman spectroscopy to study CO2 sequestration (by Carlos Pimentel, Germán Montes-Hernández, Alexander E.S. Van Driessche). I will present the results on Wednesday 20th at 12:30 in the session of Mineral-Fluid interactions. The abstract can be found here.

All the information about the conference can be found in the IMA2022 webpage. The scientific program can be found here.

Carlos Pimentel
